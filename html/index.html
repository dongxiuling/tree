  • About our Team
Take the advantage of next and third generation sequencing, more and more woody genomes have been sequenced, including Populus and Betula. However, due to lack of bioinformatics background, the utilization of is not very successful. Under such circumstance, we collected the data sets of the published tree genomes and developed an easy-to-use platform for biologists without bioinformatics skills or computing resources. The tree genomic analysis platform (TGAP) a database and analysis platform for genomic and biological information of trees. TGAP includes a RNA-seq analysis pipeline containing the following functions: quality control of the sequencing reads, alignment of reads to the transcripts references, identification of differentially expressed genes (DEGs), GO enrichment analysis for the DEGs, cluster for the DEGs, co-expression analysis for the DEGs, motif enrichment for the DEGs . In addition to the RNA-seq analysis pipeline, TGAP also provides a lot of useful tools for genome analysis, such as BLAST analysis, multiple sequence alignment, retrieve of genomic sequence, CAP3 assembler, reverse complement of sequence. User can download all the genome data sets from TGAP.
